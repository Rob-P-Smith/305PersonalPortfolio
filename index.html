I am a 3rd year student in the Green River College Software Development BAS program expecting to graduate in June 2025. My passion for technology started young; my first PC was an Apple IIc. It led to a long journey down many esoteric rabbit holes such as competitive overclocking, 3d modeling, and others.

I am a retired Army veteran with 21 years of service; 5 years deployed to various locations. I've worked at all levels of the Intelligence field, tactical, operational, and strategic. Some of my time was spent running regionally aligned programs in direct support of USSTRATCOM. My final position was mentoring National Guard and Reserve Intelligence teams to better prepare them for contingency operations.

When I'm not working on school assignments and projects, I spend my time working on custom microcontroller installations and custom motorcycle design/fabrication projects. I have a small 3D printer farm of 8 machines I use for personal projects and to support local Makers.
